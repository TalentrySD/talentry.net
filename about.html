Welcome to Talentry

Innovate. Develop. Connect. Succeed.

Talentry was founded by two individuals with over 30 years of expertise in the technology and scientific industries in Southern California. Our proven experience with Software Development, Systems Integration and Architecture, Project Delivery, Staff Augmentation, and Training have made us particularly effective at designing cost-effective solutions for our clients.

We have been successful through the “ups and downs” simply because we focused on one simple axiom: Our client have problems that need to be solved. Our solution is to solve those problems agnostically, without agenda. In today’s landscape, the solution isn’t always clear. Developing software is a road fraught with errors, and money wasted. Hiring the right people to help solve these problems has never been so challenging for our clients. Large professional services firms have been extremely costly to them, and have delivered mixed results.
